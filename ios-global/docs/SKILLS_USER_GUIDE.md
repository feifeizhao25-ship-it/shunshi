# SEASONS — User Guide to AI Skills

> Your AI companion is powered by specialized Skills — each one an expert in a different area of wellness.
> This guide explains what each Skill does and how to get the most out of them.

---

## Meet Your AI Skills

Your AI companion has 12 built-in skills. Some are always free; some require a Premium subscription.

---

## 🌅 Daily Rhythm Plan *(Free)*

**What it does:**
Generates a personalized daily wellness plan based on the current season, your life stage, and your habits.

**What you'll receive:**
- A warm morning greeting
- One key insight for the day
- 3–5 gentle daily suggestions
- A habit checklist
- An AI care note

**When to use it:**
Every morning, or whenever you want a fresh perspective on your day.

**How to access:**
From the Home screen or by asking the Companion: "What should I focus on today?"

---

## 💛 Mood First Aid *(Free)*

**What it does:**
Offers immediate emotional support when you're feeling anxious, overwhelmed, tired, or low.

**What you'll receive:**
- Immediate empathy and validation
- A gentle, tiny action you can take right now
- A follow-up question to continue the conversation if you want

**When to use it:**
When you feel stressed, anxious, sad, or just "off." It works best when you share what's actually on your mind.

**How to access:**
Tell your Companion: "I'm feeling anxious" or "I'm having a hard day."

**Safety note:**
If you share feelings that suggest a crisis, Mood First Aid will show you a special SafeCard with professional support resources. This is intentional — we take your emotional safety seriously.

## 🌙 Sleep Wind-Down *(Free)*

**What it does:**
Creates a personalized pre-sleep relaxation routine based on your sleep quality and what's keeping you awake.

**What you'll receive:**

**When to use it:**

**How to access:**
Ask your Companion: "I can't sleep" or "Help me relax before bed."

## How to Talk to Your AI Companion

SEASONS AI is designed to feel like a knowledgeable, warm friend — not a clinical assistant.

### What to try:

**Asking for help:**
> "I'm feeling tired today, what can I do?"
> "I can't sleep, help me relax."
> "What should I eat in autumn?"

**Sharing how you feel:**
> "I've been really anxious lately."
> "I had a rough day at work."

**Asking for seasonal guidance:**
> "What's the Spring Equinox about?"
> "Give me a morning routine for summer."

**Getting practical:**
> "Quick desk break, I have 3 minutes."
> "Headache relief acupoints please."

### What not to do:

- Don't ask for specific medical diagnoses
- Don't ask for prescription medication recommendations
- Don't expect instant cures — wellness is a practice, not a quick fix

---

## Tips for Better Conversations

1. **Be honest about how you feel** — the AI responds better when it has real information
2. **Ask for something small** — "one breathing exercise" beats "teach me everything about sleep"
3. **Follow up** — if one suggestion doesn't work, say so and ask for another approach
4. **Come back** — the more you interact, the better the AI understands your patterns
